Melons

A melon is any of various plants of the family Curcurbitaceae with edible, fleshy fruit. The word "melon" can refer to either the plant or specifically to the fruit. Many different cultivars have been produced particularly of musskmelons. Although the melon is a botanical fruit (speciically, a berry), some varieties may be considered vegetables rather than fruits.
